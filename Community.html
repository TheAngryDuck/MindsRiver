A happy customer

Mrs Cipher is an 85 years old elderly woman who really loves to write poems. She was brought to us with severe dementia. After 26 days of intensive treatment she was cured. Now she is happily back at her home on Caesar street. She has told us herself that she is overjoyed to have recovered and urges anyone, who is seeking help, to go to their nearest "company" clinic for treatment. At the end of the interview she remembered that before the treatment

she kept forgetting her house key.
